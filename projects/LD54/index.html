Droplet Dungeon

This is my entry for Ludum Dare 54 extra.

Instructions

Use the WASD or arrow keys to move. Use the space bar to spray water.

Once you enter a room you will need to beat the room to continue. Spray water onto the big fires to put them out. Once you have put out all the big fires the room, the room will unlock.

There are a total of four rooms, including the tutorial room.
